# EcoTrace-Supply-Chain-Verifier
EcoTrace Supply Chain Verifier is a web-based traceability system designed to improve transparency and trust in product supply chains. The platform allows manufacturers to register products, generate unique batch IDs, and log production details. As products move through the supply chain—such as distributors, warehouses, and retailers—each transit event is recorded, creating a verifiable timeline of the product’s journey.

## Problem Statement:
Consumers today have limited visibility into how the products they purchase are sourced, manufactured, and transported. Important information such as the origin of raw materials, ethical labor practices, and the environmental impact of production and logistics is often not accessible or clearly communicated. This lack of transparency makes it difficult for consumers to make informed purchasing decisions and support sustainable or ethically produced goods.

Additionally, supply chains are complex and involve multiple stakeholders, including manufacturers, distributors, warehouses, and retailers. Without a reliable system to track and verify each stage of a product’s lifecycle, it becomes challenging to ensure accountability and prevent misinformation or misleading claims about sustainability and ethical sourcing. As a result, there is a growing need for a transparent and verifiable system that allows both businesses and consumers to track the journey of products from origin to purchase. 🌱

## Proposed Solution

EcoTrace Supply Chain Verifier is a web-based traceability platform that records and verifies every stage of a product’s lifecycle, from manufacturing to the final consumer. The system allows manufacturers to create product entries and generate unique batch IDs linked to QR codes. As products move through the supply chain, logistics partners and retailers log transit events, creating a transparent and tamper-resistant record of the product journey.

Consumers can scan the QR code on a product to instantly access its origin, manufacturing details, and supply chain history. This approach improves transparency, builds consumer trust, and promotes responsible sourcing and production practices.

## Objectives

+ Increase transparency in product supply chains.
+ Allow consumers to verify product origin and lifecycle information.
+ Provide manufacturers with a traceability system for product batches.
+ Ensure data integrity through immutable logging mechanisms.
+ Support sustainable and ethical consumption decisions.
+ Digitize and simplify supply chain tracking processes.

## Core Features

+ Product & Manufacturer Management
+ Manufacturer registration and verification
+ Product creation and management dashboard
+ Batch creation with unique batch IDs
+ Supply Chain Tracking
+ Logging of product movement across the supply chain
+ Transit updates by logistics partners and warehouses
+ Real-time product lifecycle tracking
+ QR Code Traceability
+ Automatic QR code generation for each batch
+ Consumer scanning portal to view product history
+ Immutable Logging
+ Secure event logging using hash-based record chaining
+ Protection against data tampering or modification
+ Consumer Transparency Portal
+ Mobile-friendly interface for scanning product QR codes
+ Visual timeline showing product journey from origin to store

## Target Users
### Manufacturers
Factories and production units that want to maintain digital records of product batches and sourcing information.

### Supply Chain Partners
Logistics providers, warehouses, and distributors responsible for recording transit events during product movement.

### Retailers
Stores that receive products and update the system with final delivery records.

### Consumers
End users who want to verify the authenticity, sourcing, and sustainability of the products they purchase.

## SDG 12 Impact – Responsible Consumption and Production

EcoTrace directly contributes to Sustainable Development Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production by improving supply chain transparency and accountability.

The system helps:

+ Enable informed consumer decisions by providing access to product origin and lifecycle data.
+ Encourage manufacturers to adopt ethical sourcing and sustainable production practices.
+ Reduce the risk of misleading product claims or greenwashing.
+ Promote sustainable supply chain management through traceability and accountability.

By making product journeys transparent and verifiable, EcoTrace empowers both producers and consumers to participate in more responsible and sustainable consumption patterns. 🌱
